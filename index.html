About Us

Baking Frenzy started since 2008, started as a hobby baker, passionate on making delicious and beautiful cakes for family and friends. Eventually, improved the craft and learned many technique along the way. Cakes and cupcakes looked more beautiful with all the techniques acquired. All cakes were made as much as possible with all-edible decorations and were hand-made. All baked goods are also baked with fresh and natural ingredients only.
